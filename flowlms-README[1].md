# Flowitec Go & Grow LMS — Learning Management System

A full-featured **Learning Management System** built for Flowitec Group to support staff training and professional development across their African operations.

🌐 **Live Site:** [flowitecgoandgrow.com](https://flowitecgoandgrow.com)

---

## About the Project

Flowitec needed an internal learning platform to train and upskill their engineering and operations teams across Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, and South Africa. This LMS was built from scratch as a custom solution tailored to their specific training workflows.

### Key Features

- **User Authentication** — Secure login and registration system for staff and administrators
- **Course Management** — Create, organise, and manage training courses and modules
- **Learning Progress Tracking** — Monitor learner progress and completion rates
- **Admin Dashboard** — Full control panel for managing users, courses, and content
- **Responsive Design** — Accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Role-based Access** — Separate views and permissions for admins and learners
